Bio

Christian Valentin Brunn or better known by his stage name Virtual Riot, is a DJ and electronic music producer from Germany. Starting off his career in 2010, it didn't take him long to burst into the scene and have numerous Beatport chart hits including "One For All, All For One" with Razihel and "Cali Born" with Helicopter Showdown. Virtual Riot has amassed over 320,000 followers on SoundCloud and is increasing rapidly. He has released over 180 tracks, beginning with a "rock-pop" piece entitled "Wake Me Up", and most recently with his EP 'Save Yourself'. He also has exceeded 385,000 subscribers on YouTube where he uploads songs, monologues, and tutorials on music production.
